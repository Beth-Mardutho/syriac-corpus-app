Printed Edition of Hugoye Subscription

There are two options for getting a print subscription to Hugoye: Journal of Syriac Studies.

1) You can join the Bnay Beth Mardutho, for which you will receive:

-A free subscription to Hugoye for as long as you maintain your membership

-A free copy of the Gorgias Encyclopedic Dictionary of the Syriac Heritage

-Future discounts on Gorgias/Beth Mardutho Publications

2) You can subscribe just to the journal itself. The printed (bound) edition of Hugoye is available to students, individuals, and institutions at the following rates (please check one):

  1. Student $35 (after you send your payment, fax a valid student ID to +1 732-885-8908).
  2. Individual $75.
  3. Institution $145.
